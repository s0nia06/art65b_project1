My Experience with Flash Games


I don't have too many records of my Fantage or Club Penguin adventures, but I remember liking how I could log onto Fantage with my friends and we could play minigames and in-game competitions. Looking back, it was definitely a "gacha" game where you had to pay to get the really cool outfits, and "winning" items was really rare. I remember really wanting to pay to play some of the games. overall though, it was really nice that the game was free. now you have to pay a lot for video games. i remember i stopped playing flash games when i got the PC version of Minecraft for my 9th birthday.
